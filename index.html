Our Story

A kitchen where everyone belongs

Jovvy's Kitchen was born from a simple belief: cooking is one of the most generous things you can do. Whether you're a complete beginner or a seasoned home cook, our classes are designed to make you feel capable, curious, and connected.

Each session is led by Jovvy — a classically trained chef with a warm, no-fuss teaching style. No intimidation, no perfection required. Just good food, good technique, and great company.

Est. 2018
